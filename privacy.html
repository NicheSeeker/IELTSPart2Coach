Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

Our Commitment

IELTS Part 2 Coach is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe in transparency and minimal data collection. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

TL;DR: We only collect what's necessary for the app to work. Your recordings stay on your device. AI analysis is sent securely to OpenRouter. We don't track you or sell your data.

1. What Data We Collect

1.1 Audio Recordings

  • What: Voice recordings you create when practicing speaking
  • Where stored: Locally on your iPhone (not uploaded to our servers)
  • Purpose: Allow you to review your practice and receive AI feedback
  • How long: Until you manually delete them

1.2 Practice Data

  • What: Practice session metadata (date, duration, topic, AI feedback scores)
  • Where stored: Locally on your iPhone using SwiftData
  • Purpose: Track your progress and show practice history
  • How long: Until you clear history in Settings

1.3 API Key (Optional)

  • What: Your OpenRouter API key for AI analysis
  • Where stored: Securely in iOS Keychain (encrypted at rest)
  • Purpose: Enable AI feedback feature
  • How long: Until you update or remove it

2. How We Use Your Data

2.1 AI Analysis (Third-Party Service)

When you request AI feedback, your audio recording is temporarily sent to OpenRouter API (via HTTPS) for analysis using Google's Gemini AI model. This data:

  • Is not stored by us on any server
  • Is processed by OpenRouter/Gemini according to their privacy policies
  • Is transmitted securely using TLS/SSL encryption
  • Is deleted after analysis completes (not retained by OpenRouter)

Third-Party Privacy Policies:

  • OpenRouter: https://openrouter.ai/privacy
  • Google Gemini: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2.2 Speech Recognition (Optional)

If you enable "Show Transcript in Feedback" in Settings, your audio is processed by:

  • Apple Speech Recognition API (server-based for accuracy)
  • Transcripts are stored locally on your device only
  • Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

2.3 Local Notifications (Optional)

If you enable 3-day practice reminders, we schedule local notifications on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

3. What We DON'T Do

We want to be crystal clear about what we don't do with your data:

  • ❌ We don't track your usage or behavior
  • ❌ We don't use analytics SDKs (no Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • ❌ We don't show ads or collect data for advertising
  • ❌ We don't sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ We don't require account creation or collect personal information
  • ❌ We don't store recordings on servers (everything stays local)
  • ❌ We don't share data except for AI analysis (as described above)

4. Your Rights & Control

4.1 Access & Export

You can export all your practice data (sessions, feedback, progress) as a JSON file from Settings → Data Management → Export Data.

4.2 Delete Data

You have full control to delete your data:

  • Individual sessions: Swipe left in History view
  • All history: Settings → Data Management → Clear All History
  • App deletion: Deleting the app removes all local data permanently

4.3 Disable Features

  • Turn off AI analysis: Don't tap "Get AI feedback" button
  • Turn off transcripts: Settings → Speech Recognition → Toggle off
  • Turn off reminders: Settings → Notifications → Toggle off

5. How We Protect Your Data

  • Local Storage: All recordings and practice data use iOS default encryption
  • Keychain: API key stored in iOS Keychain (industry-standard secure storage)
  • Network: All API requests use HTTPS (TLS 1.3 encryption)
  • No Cloud Sync: We don't sync data to iCloud or external servers

6. Children's Privacy

IELTS Part 2 Coach does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is designed for IELTS test takers, typically ages 16+. We don't require age verification because we don't collect personal information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as the app evolves. We'll notify users of significant changes via:

  • App Store update notes
  • In-app notification (if applicable)
  • Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page

Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
